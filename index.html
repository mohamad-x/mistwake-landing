The Problem

Regular alarms shock you awake.

Loud alarms work, but they are aggressive. MistWake is designed for people who want a more peaceful, physical, and modern wake-up experience.

🔊

Harsh sounds

Traditional alarms rely on noise first, which can make mornings feel stressful.

😴

Easy to ignore

Phone alarms are too easy to snooze, silence, or sleep through.

💧

No physical cue

MistWake adds a light mist cue to help your body notice it is time to wake.

How It Works

A calmer wake-up sequence.

1

Set your wake time

Use the front/top controls to set your preferred alarm time.

2

Fill the compact reservoir

Add water through the top fill cap and check the level window.

3

Wake with mist + sound

At your alarm time, MistWake activates a gentle mist cue with alarm feedback.
